Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Welcome to The Core Workshop. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly from you. When you use our service through the Pi Browser and choose to submit a project, we use the Pi SDK to access the following information:

  • Pi User ID (UID): A unique identifier for your Pi account.
  • Pi Username: The username associated with your Pi account.

We do not collect or store your email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

The Pi User ID and Username are used solely for the purpose of attributing the projects you submit to your Pi account. This allows the community to see who created a particular project.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Firebase for its database. This service stores the project data and your Pi User ID and Username to power the app's functionality.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Pi Browser's in-app chat.
